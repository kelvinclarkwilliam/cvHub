HISTORY OF ARDEE

A viral appearance on Russ Millions and Tion Wayne 's "Body [Remix]"
landed both ArrDee and the track a spot in the history books: it became the first drill song to reach number one on the U.K.
charts, due in no small part to the Brighton MC's iconic verse.
He released his debut mixtape, Pier Pressure, in 2022.
